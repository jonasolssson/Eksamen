Stuff you didn´t know about brownies!

Guess you didn't know the first brownie came around from a request for a dessert more suitable for ladies huh? We didn't either. Diving deep into the history of the most delicious treat known to mankind has been tickling our brains for a while, and we thought we should share some of our findings with you!

The origin of the brownie

As mentioned the brownie came to be from a request of a bit of an old-fashioned style. Bertha Palmer, whose husband owned the Palmer House Hotel wanted to add a new sort of dessert to the hotel's menu. A cake-like confection was requested. Palmer requested the dessert in question to be compact and packable, preferably to fit in a lunch box! The result was the brownie, basically a chocolate cake made with the abscence of baking powder.

Stuff you definetly didn´t know about brownies:

  • The first cook-book referenced brownie did not contain chocolate
  • Brownies first appeared in cookbooks in 1904
  • The three most popular styles of brownies among americans are:
    1. Macadamia Brownies
    2. Chunky Blond Brownies
    3. Pot Brownies(!)
